Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Introduction

At Zenvexa API, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our API services, website, and related platforms.

By using Zenvexa API, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations.

Data Collection

Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when:

  • Creating an account (name, email, company)
  • Making API requests (request data, parameters)
  • Contacting support (messages, tickets)
  • Purchasing services (billing information)

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, device info, timestamps
  • Usage Data: API calls, response times, error rates
  • Location Data: Approximate location from IP address
  • Cookies: Session management, preferences, analytics
Data Type Purpose Retention Period
Account Information Authentication, service delivery Account lifetime + 30 days

Billing Information Payment processing, tax compliance 7 years (legal requirement)
Log Data Security, performance monitoring 90 days

Data Usage

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain our API services
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Improve service performance and reliability
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent security incidents

Data Processing Legal Bases

We process personal data based on:

  • Contract: To provide services you requested
  • Legitimate Interests: Service improvement, security
  • Consent: Marketing communications (opt-in)
  • Legal Obligation: Tax, regulatory compliance

Cookies and Tracking

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used with your consent only

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (block/delete cookies)
  • Our cookie preference center
  • Third-party opt-out tools

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent some features from working properly.

Data Protection

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for accounts
  • Role-based access controls
  • 24/7 security monitoring

Data Breach Response

In case of data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Provide detailed incident report
  • Implement corrective measures
  • Comply with regulatory notification requirements

Data Sharing

Third-Party Processors

We share data only with trusted processors:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics services (with anonymization)
  • Customer support tools

Legal Disclosures

We may disclose data when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Respond to lawful requests

We will notify you of legal requests unless prohibited by law.

International Data Transfers

We process data globally using:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data
  • Adequacy decisions where available
  • Encryption during transfer and storage
  • Data localization options for enterprise customers

Your Rights

Data Subject Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request data deletion (with limitations)
  • Portability: Export your data
  • Restriction: Limit processing in certain cases
  • Objection: Object to processing for direct marketing
  • Consent Withdrawal: Revoke consent anytime

Exercising Rights

Submit requests via email or your account dashboard. We respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Active account + 30 days
  • API logs: 30 days (security logs: 90 days)
  • Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Support tickets: 2 years

After retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children's data. If you believe we have children's data, contact us for immediate deletion.

Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes include:

  • Updated "Last updated" date
  • Email notification for material changes
  • 30-day notice for significant changes
  • Consent required for new uses
