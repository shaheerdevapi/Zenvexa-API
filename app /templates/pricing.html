Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

What happens if I exceed my request limit?

Your requests will be temporarily throttled. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional request credits to continue using the service.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

Is there a commitment period?

No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time.

How do I track my API usage?

Every plan includes access to a detailed analytics dashboard where you can monitor your requests, response times, and usage patterns in real-time.
